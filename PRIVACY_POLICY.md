# Privacy Policy for Urdu Writer

**Last updated:** [Date]

Thank you for using Urdu Writer ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy is to inform you about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

## Information Collection and Use

Urdu Writer is an offline application. We do not collect, store, use, or share any personally identifiable information from our users. All documents, custom fonts, and other data you create or import are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted or made accessible to us or any third party.

## Permissions

Urdu Writer requests the following permissions for the sole purpose of enabling app functionality:

*   **Storage (`READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE` and `WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE`):** These permissions are required to allow you to save your documents on your device's storage and to import custom fonts from your device. All file operations are performed locally. We do not access, read, or upload any of your personal files or data for any other purpose.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, your information is as secure as your device. The security of your data is entirely within your control.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository issues.
